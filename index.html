Our Process

From the ground up

Every drop of OlumilliPure passes through our complete purification journey before it reaches your hands.

1
Source extraction

Drawn from a certified underground aquifer — naturally low in contaminants from the start.

2
Multi-stage filtration

Sand, carbon, resin, and reverse osmosis remove all impurities, chlorine, and heavy metals.

3
UV sterilisation

Ultraviolet treatment eliminates any remaining microbial and bacterial contaminants.

4
Sealed & certified

Bottled under GMP standards, lab-tested, then NAFDAC and SON approved before distribution.
